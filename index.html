About Us

Organic India is a multi-national company founded in 1997 by couple Bharat Mitra and Bhavani Lev, in Lucknow, India, that produces halal certified organic herbal and Ayurvedic health products. The company is most known for their line of organically grown tulsi teas, which are sold in India, the US, Canada, and the UK.

ORGANIC INDIA is committed to being a trustworthy and innovative global leader by providing genuine True Wellness products. Our advanced processing methods and dehydration technologies ensure that our herbs retain their maximum level of potency for the highest quality, most effective, pure and natural True Wellness products available in the market today.
